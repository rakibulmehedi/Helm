# Freelancer User Interview Questions

Use these questions during generative or evaluative interviews with target users (Bangladeshi freelancers).

## 1. Cashflow Anxiety & Context
- "Tell me about a time recently when you felt stressed about money, even though you had work."
- "When you want to buy something non-essential (like a gadget or a nice dinner), how do you check if you can afford it?"
- "Do you ever mentally count money that a client hasn't paid you yet?"

## 2. Pending Payments & The Pipeline
- "How do you currently keep track of who owes you what?"
- "Have you ever experienced a delayed payment that forced you to change your spending plans? Walk me through it."
- "How do you separate your 'business' money from your 'personal' money?"

## 3. Current Tools & Workarounds
- "What apps or spreadsheets are you using right now to track income/expenses?"
- "What is the most frustrating part about your current system?"
- "How often do you actually update your tracker? Daily? Weekly? When you remember?"

## 4. The Safe-to-Spend Metric
- "If an app told you exactly what was 'Safe to Spend' after accounting for your taxes, upcoming bills, and a safety buffer, how would that change your daily decisions?"
- "What deductions would be absolutely necessary for you to trust that number?" (e.g., specific software subs, family commitments).

## 5. Friction & Trust
- "If you had to manually log every project and payment into an app, how long would you stick with it?"
- "What would make an app feel secure enough for you to input your real financial numbers?"

## 6. Feature Priority
- "If you could wave a magic wand and solve one financial tracking problem instantly, what would it be?"
- "Rank these from most to least important to you: 1) Knowing who owes you money, 2) Knowing exactly what you can spend today safely, 3) Tracking your past expenses in pie charts."
