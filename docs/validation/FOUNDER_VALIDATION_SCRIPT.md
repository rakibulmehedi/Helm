# Founder Validation Script

**Purpose:** To guide the founder during 1-on-1 testing sessions with 5–10 real freelancers. The goal is to validate trust, comprehension of the Safe-to-Spend metric, and manual entry friction without bias.

## 1. Introduction (2 mins)
- "I'm building a tool to help freelancers manage cashflow anxiety."
- "I want you to try it out. There are no right or wrong actions. Think out loud as you use it."
- *Do not explain the Safe-to-Spend formula yet. Let them discover it.*

## 2. Testing Protocol & Tasks (5 mins)
Ask the user to perform the following tasks while you observe:
1. **Task 1:** "Add a payment you recently received."
2. **Task 2:** "Add a payment you are waiting for (invoiced but unpaid)."
3. **Task 3:** "Add a project you just started (expected income)."
4. **Task 4:** "Look at the main dashboard. Tap on the big number at the top."

## 3. Observation Checklist (Silent for the founder)
- [ ] Did they pause or get confused adding an income status (Received/Pending/Expected)?
- [ ] Did they notice the big "Safe to Spend" number change?
- [ ] Did they successfully tap the hero section to see the breakdown?
- [ ] Did they complain about having to type everything in manually?
- [ ] **Confusion Signal:** Did they ask "Why isn't my pending money in the total?"
- [ ] **Success Signal:** Did they say "Ah, this tells me what I actually have."

## 4. Post-Test Interview Questions (3 mins)
- **Comprehension:** "What do you think that big 'Safe to Spend' number means?"
- **Trust:** "If that number said you had ৳5,000 left to spend, would you trust it enough to buy something for ৳4,000 today?"
- **Friction:** "How did it feel to add those items manually? Would you do this every time you got a new client?"
- **Emotional Reaction:** "How does seeing your pending vs. received money separated make you feel?"
- **Willingness to Pay:** "If this app automatically synced your bank and Upwork data to give you this exact number every morning, what would you pay for it per month?"

## 5. Debrief & Notes
*After the user leaves, record red flags, green flags, and direct quotes.*
